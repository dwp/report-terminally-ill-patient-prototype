You will need to complete an SR1 form for a patient who has between 6-12 months to live

You can either complete the form electronically or send us a paper copy digitally. 

Returning by email

You can get the electronic version of the SR1 form and fee form by emailing

It must be from your current secure email or group secure email. Secure email addresses end in @nhs.net, @nhs.uk, @wales.nhs.uk, @nhs.scot and ex.gov.uk

We need to know:

  • your secure email address or addresses
  • your surgery/ hospital/ office postal address and telephone number
  • a list of medical professionals who will use the same secure email address 
  • whether you need the SR1 form and the fee form, or the SR1 form only

No signature is required.

Returning by post

You can get a form by either emailing dwpcst@theapsgroup.com or fax to 0161 495 4840 

We need to know:

  • your surgery, hospital or office postal address and telephone number
  • a list of medical professionals who will use the same secure email address 
  • whether you need the SR1 form and the fee form, or the SR1 form only

The address to send the completed form is:

UC/ESA

Make sure the form is signed and use a separate envelope for each patient.
