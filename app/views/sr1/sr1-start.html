How to get an SR1 form

This is for a patient who may have 6 to 12 months left to live.

It is for Universal Credit (UC) or New Style Employment Support (ESA) claims only.

How to get a PDF version of the SR1 form

To get a copy email, e-SR1@dwp.gov.uk via a secure email address.

Secure email addresses end in @nhs.net, @nhs.uk, @wales.nhs.uk, @nhs.scot and @gov.uk.

Tell us in the email:

  • your surgery, hospital or office postal address and telephone number
  • a list of medical professionals who may complete the SR1 forms
  • whether you need the SR1 form and the fee form, or the SR1 form only

How to get paper copies of the SR1 form

You can order a book of blank paper copies

Tell us on headed paper:

  • your surgery, hospital or office postal address and telephone number
  • a list of medical professionals who may complete the SR1 forms

Call the HH Global Limited helpline on 0333 016 9656 if you have any questions.
