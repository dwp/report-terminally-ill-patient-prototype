Report a terminally ill patient

Use this service to tell the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) that:

  • a patient has a severe progressive condition

and

  • you would not be surprised if they were to live for less than 6-12 months
You will not face any negative consequences from the factual information you supply, for example if your patient lives longer than 12 months.

Also use this service to download a copy of the SR1 or DS1500 to save securely for your patient's records.
