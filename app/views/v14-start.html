Report a terminally ill patient (DS1500)

Use this service to tell the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) that:

  • a patient has a severe progressive condition

    • and

  • you would not be surprised if they were to live for less than 6 months
You will not face any negative consequences from the factual information you supply, for example if your patient lives longer than 6 months.

Also use this service to download a copy of the DS1500 to save securely for your patient's records.
