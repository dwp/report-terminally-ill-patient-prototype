Prototypes for Design Team for User Testing

This section is for the Design Team to test new features with users. This section is not for the Development Team. The Development Team should refer to Prototype for Developers section.

Prototype v1 is designed with several questions per page
Prototype v2 is designed with 1 question per page
Prototype v3 is designed with all questions on one single page. Please note that this prototype does not have check your answers functionality due to fear of duplication
Prototype v4 is designed with all questions on one single page. Since a check your answers functionality is not recommended to avoid duplication, Prototype v4 tests a YES/NO Radio component at the end of the form stating: have you checked all the information you have entered for errors?
Prototype v5 is designed with all questions on one single page. Further user research has indicated that users may actually want a check your answers page as it is a chance to "preview" the application before its submitted. Therefore this prototype contains "check your answers" functionality
Prototype v6 experiments with some new minor designs and components to aid the flow of the service, with the aim of A/B Testing them.
Prototype v7 experiments with builds from a team workshop on Friday 8th Jan.
Prototype v8 implements with builds from a series of UR sessions with medical professionals starting 2nd February 2021.
! Warning 'Prototypes for Design Team for User Testing' are not for production!
