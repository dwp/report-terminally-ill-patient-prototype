If the problem persists and you need to complete a DS1500 you can complete form DS1500 and the fee form electronically and submit it to us by using the NHS mail system or from a nhs.wales.uk email address. Please provide the following information by e-mail from your current NHS.net or nhs.wales.uk email address:

  • your NHS.net or nhs.wales.uk e-mail address or addresses
  • your surgery/hospital/office postal address and telephone number
  • a list of medical professionals using the same NHS.net or nhs.wales.uk email
  • please advise if you require the DS1500 and DS1500 fee form, or the DS1500 only
  • send these details to: pip.e-ds1500@dwp.gov.uk
  • Do not encrypt any part of the email. Any email from a non-NHS.net or nhs.wales.uk account will be automatically deleted
  • if you do not have an NHS.net or nhs.wales.uk email address, contact your local IT helpdesk to request an account
