Sorry, we cannot log you in to the service

Only specific job roles are authorised to complete an SR1 form.

These are:

  • GP
  • hospital consultant
  • specialty doctor
  • hospice doctor
  • senior nurse employed at Band 7 or 8, who may be working as a clinical nurse specialist, advanced nurse practitioner or similar

Your job role is checked against the job role on your NHS smart card. If you should have access to this service, make sure your smart card is correct. If it’s wrong, your IT support team will be able to help.

If you do not have an account to log incidents with the NHS National Service Deskcall 0300 303 5035.

What you can do if the problem continues

You can complete PDF versions of the SR1 and the fee claim forms online. They do not need to be signed, but you’ll need to return them to us from a secure email address. Any email that does not come from a secure email address will be automatically deleted.

Secure email addresses end in:

  • @nhs.net
  • @nhs.uk
  • @nhs.scot
  • @nhs.wales.uk
  • @gov.uk

Send the request for a PDF version of the SR1 form, and fee claim form if needed, from your secure email address to form.e-sr1@dwp.gov.uk.

Please include your:

  • surgery/hospital/postal address
  • phone number

    • Do not encrypt any part of the email.
