If you need to complete an SR1 form now

You can complete PDF versions of the SR1 and the fee claim forms online. They do not need to be signed, but you’ll need to return them to us from a secure email address. Any email that does not come from a secure email address will be automatically deleted.

Secure email addresses end in:

  • @nhs.net
  • @nhs.uk
  • @nhs.scot
  • @nhs.wales.uk
  • @gov.uk

Send the request for a PDF version of the SR1 form, and fee claim form if needed, from your secure email address to form.e-sr1@dwp.gov.uk.

Please include your:

  • surgery/hospital/postal address
  • phone number

Do not encrypt any part of the email.
