You will need to complete an SR1 form for your patient who may have 6 to 12 months left to live

You can either complete the form electronically or send us a paper copy.Â 

Our preferred method of return is via email.

You can complete an electronic version of the SR1 form and fee form and submit it to us at e-SR1@dwp.gov.ukvia a secure email system.

Secure email addresses end in @nhs.net, @nhs.uk, @wales.nhs.uk, @nhs.scot and ex.gov.uk

Do not encrypt any part of the email. Any email that does not come from a secure email account will be automatically deleted.

Other methods of return

There are 2 options:

  • give the SR1 form to your patient (or their representative who is an appointee or a person who has power of attorney) who asked you to complete it. You will need to return the fee form separately to us
  • send the completed SR1 form and fee form if you have one to your local Jobcentre Plus office

Only original signed copies of will be accepted.

Use a separate envelope for each patient and do not staple documents together.

How to get a form

You can order a book of paper copies or get an electronic version by emailing SR1@dwp.gov.uk.

Secure email addresses end in @nhs.net, @nhs.uk, @wales.nhs.uk, @nhs.scot and @[x].gov.uk

We need to know:

  • your surgery, hospital or office postal address and telephone number
  • whether you need the SR1 form and the fee form, or the SR1 form only
  • A list of medical professionals who may complete the SR1 forms
