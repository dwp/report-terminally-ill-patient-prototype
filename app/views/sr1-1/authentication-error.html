Sorry, we cannot log you in to the service

This might be due to a technical problem.

Sign in to the NHS Spine Portal and try launching the SR1 service again. If you’re still not able to log in, you can complete a PDF version of the SR1 form and the fee claim form which you can email back to us.

The PDF versions do not need a signature, but you’ll need to return them from an nhs.net or an nhs.wales.uk email address. Emails from any other addresses will be automatically deleted. If you do not have one of these email addresses, ask your local IT helpdesk to provide one.

How to get a PDF version of the SR1 form

You’ll need to send us the following information:

  • your work address and phone number
  • your nhs.net or nhs.wales.uk email address
  • a list of medical professionals using the same email address who can complete the SR1 form
  • whether you need just the SR1 form or the fee claim form as well

More information about this issue

Email this information with your request to form.e-sr1@dwp.gov.uk.

Do not encrypt any part of the email.
